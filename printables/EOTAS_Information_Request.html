EOTAS Information Request

When considering EOTAS for your child, you may wish to ask the local authority some starter questions. Use this template as a checklist to ensure you gather all the key information.

  • What arrangements can you provide for education outside of school?
  • How many hours per week will be offered and how will those hours be allocated between subjects and therapies?
  • What qualifications or accreditation can be achieved through EOTAS?
  • How will social interaction opportunities be provided?
  • What transport arrangements will be made (if off‑site provision is offered)?
  • How will progress be monitored and reviewed?

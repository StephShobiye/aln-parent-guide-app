IDP Review Request Letter

This template helps you request a review of your child’s Individual Development Plan (IDP). Describe how your child’s needs have changed and explain why a review is necessary. Personalise the brackets with your information.

[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

Dear [Headteacher/ALNCo],

I am writing to request an urgent review of my child’s Individual Development Plan. Since the last review there have been significant changes in my child’s needs and provision. I have attached supporting evidence and would like to meet to discuss amendments to the IDP.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]

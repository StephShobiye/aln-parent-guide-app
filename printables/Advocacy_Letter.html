Advocacy Letter

This template can be used to advocate for changes when your child’s placement is unsafe or failing. It highlights safeguarding concerns and requests action from the school or local authority.

[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

Dear [Headteacher/ALN Manager],

I am writing to express my serious concerns about the current placement of my child, [Child’s Name]. There have been multiple incidents of [describe incidents] which have placed my child at risk and impacted their wellbeing. I request an urgent meeting to agree interim measures and to review the IDP. Please confirm in writing the steps that will be taken to ensure my child’s safety.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter. I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]

This printable is provided for guidance only and does not constitute legal advice. (Release 4.0.0)

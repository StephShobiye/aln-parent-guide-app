Tribunal Appeal Letter

Use this template to lodge an appeal with the Education Tribunal for Wales. You must usually appeal within 8 weeks of receiving the local authority’s decision.

[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

To: Education Tribunal for Wales

I wish to appeal the decision of [Local Authority] dated [Date of Decision] regarding [brief description of decision]. I believe the decision is incorrect because [reasons].

I enclose the following documents in support of my appeal:

  • Copy of the decision letter
  • My child’s IDP and recent review documents
  • Reports and evidence supporting my case

Please acknowledge receipt of this appeal and provide information about next steps.

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]

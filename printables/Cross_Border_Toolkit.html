Cross‑Border Toolkit

This toolkit provides guidance when your child’s needs can only be met by a school or provision outside your local authority’s area or across a national border.

Gather evidence

  • Obtain reports from professionals recommending an out‑of‑area placement.
  • Document attempts to make the current or proposed placement work and why it failed.
  • Identify suitable schools or services elsewhere and gather information on their provision.

Present your case

  • Explain why local provision is unsuitable (distance, specialism, safety).
  • Describe how the out‑of‑area placement meets your child’s needs.
  • Request that transport be provided if required.

Next steps

  • Submit your evidence to the local authority and request that the out‑of‑area placement be named in the IDP.
  • If refused, follow the complaints process and consider a tribunal appeal.
  • Keep records of all correspondence and decisions.

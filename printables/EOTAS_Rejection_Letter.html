EOTAS Rejection Letter

If the local authority refuses to arrange EOTAS, you can challenge the decision. This template helps you set out your reasons and request that the LA reconsider.

[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

Dear [Local Authority ALN Manager],

I am writing to appeal the decision to refuse Education Otherwise Than At School (EOTAS) for my child, [Child’s Name]. I believe the proposed school placement cannot meet my child’s needs because [explain reasons]. I request a reconsideration of the decision and provide the following evidence in support: [list reports, incidents, etc.].

Thank you for reviewing this request. I look forward to your prompt response.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]

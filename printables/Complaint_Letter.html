Complaint Letter

This template can be used to submit a formal complaint about an ALN decision or placement. It helps you set out the facts clearly and request a resolution.

[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

Dear [Complaints Officer],

I am writing to make a formal complaint about the decision of [School/Local Authority] on [Date of Decision]. I believe the decision is incorrect because [explain reasons, referencing the ALN Code or legal duties]. I request that the decision be reviewed and that the following action be taken: [state desired outcome].

Please confirm receipt of this complaint and let me know when I can expect a full response.

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]

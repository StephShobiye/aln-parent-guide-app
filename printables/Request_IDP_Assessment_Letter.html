Request an IDP Assessment

This letter template helps you formally request an Individual Development Plan (IDP) assessment for your child.

[Your Name]
[Address]
[Date]

Dear [Headteacher/ALNCo],

I am writing to request an assessment of my child, [Child’s Name], for an Individual Development Plan. I believe an assessment is necessary because [briefly describe your child’s needs and the impact on learning]. Please confirm that my request has been received and let me know the next steps in the process.

Thank you for your consideration.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]

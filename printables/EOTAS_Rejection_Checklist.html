EOTAS Rejection Checklist

Use this checklist to prepare your appeal if the local authority refuses EOTAS.

  • ☐ Request the decision in writing with reasons.
  • ☐ Gather evidence of why the school placement is unsuitable (reports, incident logs, medical letters).
  • ☐ List alternative provisions you have considered and why they are not appropriate.
  • ☐ Refer to the child’s IDP and identify unmet needs.
  • ☐ Draft a letter using the **EOTAS Rejection Letter** template.
  • ☐ Consider seeking independent advice or mediation.
  • ☐ Note the appeal deadline (usually 8 weeks) for the Education Tribunal.

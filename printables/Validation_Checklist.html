Validation Checklist

Use this checklist before important meetings (e.g., IDP reviews, mediation, tribunal hearings) to ensure you have everything you need:

  • ☐ Latest IDP and previous review notes
  • ☐ Evidence of need: reports, letters, diaries
  • ☐ Log of provision delivered and missed sessions
  • ☐ Questions you want to ask and points you want to make
  • ☐ Copy of relevant legislation or guidance (ALN Code)
  • ☐ Support person or advocate (if needed)

This printable is provided for guidance only and does not constitute legal advice. (Release 4.0.0)
